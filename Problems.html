Known problems¶

multiphonon S(Q,E) may fail at low temperature¶

This calculation involves computation of factors like exp(E/kBT) and could diverge if E is too large compare to kBT. It fails when, for example, when input DOS has an energy range up to 50meV, and temperature is 3 Kelvin, and multiphonon S(Q,E) spectrum is calculated up to 10th order.

multiphonon S(Q,E) may fail if phonon DOS has too many data points¶

Less than 1000 data points should be fine. Otherwise, may be out of memory.
