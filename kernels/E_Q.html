Analytical E(Q)Â¶

This kernel scatters neutrons according to a \(S(|\vec{Q}|,E)=S(|\vec{Q}|) \delta(E-E(|\vec{Q}|))\) input. Both \(S(|\vec{Q}|)\) and \(E(|\vec{Q}|)\) are analytical functions.

Parameters:

  • Qmin: Minimum momentum transfer
  • Qmax: Minimum momentum transfer
  • E_Q: E(Q) expression. for example: 30+5*sin(Q)
  • S_Q: S(Q) expression. for example: 1

Example:

<E_Q_Kernel
   E_Q="30+5*sin(Q)"
   S_Q="1"
   Qmin="0./angstrom"
   Qmax="10./angstrom"
   />
